Your Medications

Prednisone 20mg

Belongs to a group of medicines known as corticosteroids

Also Called: Apo-Prednisone速. Prednisone速

What Is It Used For?

Prednisone is a medicine used to treat or prevent many conditions associated with inflammation. It is in a class of medicines known as corticosteroids, which is similar to what your body naturally makes. It works by calming the immune system.

Common Side effects

  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Headache
  • Muscle weakness, feeling tired
  • Changes in mood or behaviour
  • Mood swings, irritability, anxiety, bad dreams
  • Problems with your stomach such as stomach pain, blood in your stool, or dark coloured stool.

Diclofenac 50mg

Diclofenac is one of a group of medicines known as non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs).

Also Called: Voltaren速 Diclofenac (Dr Reddy's)速

What Is It Used For?

It is used to treat different types of pain such as muscle pain, dental pain, migraine and pain resulting from injury or after surgery.

Diclofenac also helps to ease redness and swelling caused by injury.

It blocks the inflammation process in your body and in this way eases swelling and pain.

Common Side effects

  • Heartburn
  • Indigestion
  • Stomach discomfort
  • Serious stomach problems such as really bad stomach pain, blood in the stool or black stools, cough or vomit up blood or dark coloured vomit.
  • Allergic reaction such as skin rash, itching, swelling of the lips, face, and mouth or difficulty breathing.
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath or trouble breathing.
  • Weakness in one part or side of the body.
  • Slurred speech.
